INFORMATICS ENGINEERING IS THE CRUCIBLE WHERE THE FUTURE IS FORGED

Exploring a broad spectrum of computer, information, and energy systems, Informatics Engineering unites top-tier faculty and students to push boundaries and foster innovation.
